Facilities

School Facilites

The School Facilities include the School Bus, Playground, Healthy Canteen, Positive Learning and more...

School Bus

The school Bus Facility is an optional facility and it cannot be claimed as a matter of right by any student / parent.

Playground

It is a place designed to provide an environment for children that facilitates play, typically outdoors.

Healthy Canteen

If your child wants lunch from the school canteen, healthy choices include multigrain sandwiches, soups, curries, fruit salads and yoghurt.

Positive Learning

We create a strong sense of togetherness and unity within the classes.
